Explore the world with us

Our objective is to change the industry

We decided to build this company because we saw the high demand for travelling. We know that it costs a lot of money unfortunately and many people cannot afford to see the beauty of our planet.

Our goal is to offer a new way to travel. At Kawah Villas, we want to give people the opportunity to explore the world without spending ridiculous amounts of money.

Our Mission

Create luxury experiences

By building a membership business model, we are giving the opportunity to people to manage their vacations themselves, without any stress.

We know that planning trips can be a headache sometimes, especially when you have to manage your children's schedule, your partner's and your's. Overall, it comes with big responsibilities, but travelling is supposed to be fun and relaxing, not stressful! This is the problem we are solving.

With our three different memberships, people can plan their trips in advance without spending huge one-time fees, they have unlimited access to all our different villas and they can bring along their loved ones to share the experience.

Our Story

The search for a revolution, for people, industry, and the world.

Nowadays, everything is moving fast and sometimes we don't even realize it. It's important to take a step back, once in a while, and rethink about our life and goals. Unfortunately for most people, it is very difficult to find the time to do it.

This is why a membership business model is much easier for many industries. It brings a peace of mind and a sense of security.

Our brain is already filled with thousands of stimuli every second and we cannot remember to do everything. This is why we are here, our members can wake up one day and remember that they can book a trip at any moment if they want to. This is what we call freedom of travelling.
