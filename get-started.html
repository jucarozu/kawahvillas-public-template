Choose the membership that fits your needs

Depending on your group size, you need to select a membership that will meet your needs. We encourage you to take the time to look carefully at all the membership options. However, you can contact us with any questions or concerns.

Single Membership

$90

/month
  • Includes one active trip at a time. When you check out, you can book your next trip.
  • Invite a guest to share the experience (Additional guest fees will be incurred).
  • Exclusive access to our destinations.
  • 5 days and 4 nights.
  • No contract needed, cancel at any time.

Duo Membership

$150

/month
  • Includes one active trip at a time for two people. When you check out, you can book your next trip.
  • Invite a guest to share the experience (Additional guest fees will be incurred).

  • 5 days and 4 nights (Extensions available).
  • No contract needed, cancel at any time.

/month

  • No contract needed, cancel at any time.
